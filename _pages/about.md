## Research Interests

My research focuses on algorithms and systems for Big Data Management and Data Science. Current interests include:

- **Causality-aware Data+AI analytics:** causal provenance query, causal property graphs, and scalable intervention analysis over large graph data.
- **Graph-based LLM systems:** graph-based RAG, structured retrieval, graph memory, and agentic retrieval for knowledge-intensive tasks.
- **Large (Language) Models for Data:** LLM-powered and pretrained methods for dataset search, latency prediction, cardinality estimation, and DBMS testing.
- **Graph mining and scalable graph algorithms:** densest subgraph discovery, community search, clique counting/listing, temporal graph analytics, and graph edit distance estimation.
